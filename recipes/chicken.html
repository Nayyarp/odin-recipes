Braised Chicken Thighs

Chicken is one of the most popular and easiest dishes to make! There are SO MANY ways to make chicken that is tasty, nutritious, and healthy!

This recipe calls for baking chicken thighs in the oven after they have been coated with some salted butter. This will ensure the chicken is glistening, savoury, and most of all, super tasty without being dried out!

Ingredients you'll need

  • Fresh chicken thighs
  • Seasoning and herbs
  • Salted butter
  • lemon juice

The cooking process

  1. Set pan on stove to medium-high heat
  2. Add the seasoned chicken to the pan
  3. toss in some butter and add some lemon juice
  4. cook until the chicken skin becomes golden brown
